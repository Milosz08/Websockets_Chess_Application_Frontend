Unsubscribe the newsletter

To unsubscribe from our newsletter, enter your email address in the form opposite. A message will be sent to this address allowing you to unsubscribe. You can unsubscribe in two ways:
  • by clicking on the link in the email sent to your address
  • or you can manually enter the access token sent to the email address you provided.
When you remove your email address from the newsletter subscription, you will receive feedback in the window below. If you have an account, you can also change your advanced sharing settings via this link.
